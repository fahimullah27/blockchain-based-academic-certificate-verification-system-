How It Works

Three Simple Steps

Getting started takes less than 30 seconds.

01
Upload Your Certificate
Drag and drop your certificate file or click to select. We support PDF, DOCX, JPG, and PNG formats up to 10MB.
02
Hash is Generated
Our system computes a unique SHA-256 fingerprint of your certificate and stores it securely in the database.
03
Verify Anytime
Upload the same certificate later to verify its authenticity. Any tampering will produce a different hash — instantly detected.
